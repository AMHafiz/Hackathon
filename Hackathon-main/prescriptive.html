British Columbia

British Columbia (BC) is located on the west coast and is famous for its stunning natural beauty, from mountains to the Pacific Ocean. Its major city, Vancouver, is known for its diversity and outdoor lifestyle.

Nunavut

Nunavut is Canada's largest and northernmost territory. It is sparsely populated and primarily inhabited by Inuit communities. It offers dramatic Arctic landscapes and a unique cultural heritage.

Quebec

Quebec is the only Canadian province with a predominantly French-speaking population. Known for its vibrant culture and history, it includes cities like Montreal and Quebec City.

Alberta

Alberta is a province known for its natural beauty including the Rocky Mountains and Banff National Park. It also has a strong energy sector and vibrant cities like Calgary and Edmonton.
